What to Eat at UGA

Make some noise for UGA Dining!

Are you a hungry UGA student? Do you have a mealplan?

If you answered yes to both of these, then this wepbage is for you! Here you will find reviews for all the wonderful places on campus you can use your mealplan. Use our reviews of UGA's dining halls and restaraunts to pick the right place to grab some grub! The eateries are evaluated by the following criteria: dining hall vs. restaraunt vs. grab and go, location, type of food, and cost. Each dining option is also given a (subjective) satisfaction rating on a 1-5 scale.

UGA has 4 main dining halls and various restaraunts scattered throughout campus. The 4 main dining halls are Bolton, Snelling, ECV (Joe Frank Harris) Commons, and Ogelthorpe (O-house). Restaraunts include Chick-Fil-A, Panda Express, Niche Pizza, Barberitos, Einsteins Bagels & Coffee, Starbucks, a UGA Taco Truck, Jittery Joe's Coffee, Au Bon Pain, and many more! There are also express cafes featuring grab-and-go items and snacks made by UGA.

We will review here the main dining halls, the chain restaruants, the food truck, and two of the cafe/market place shops.

Notes about the Table

Helpful Hints for making use of our reviews!

  • Get the TAPINGO app, as mentioned in the Chick-Fil-A and Starbucks reviews! It's UGA's very own order-ahead app that lets you skip the lines at most of the restaraunts.
  • The "Food Served" collumn is overview information. There are many more menu items for each location.
  • "MP" indicates Meal Plan dollars may be used to purchase food here, "MP*" means you can use Meal Plan dollars on only certain items here.
  • Wait Times are approximate. Do not come after us if you find them to be shorter or longer than listed here.
  • Review comments are the subjective opinions of the author of this webpage and her friends. Do not sue us if you eat at Chick-Fil-A and find it a less than 5-star dining experience...
